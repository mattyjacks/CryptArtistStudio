A platform that allows users to upload multiple financial documents—such as PDFs and images of bank statements, transaction histories, W-8BEN forms, and other tax-related records—and uses AI to automatically process and organize the data to complete approximately 90% of the tax preparation workflow. The system intelligently extracts financial information, categorizes transactions, and links supporting documents (for example, associating specific W-8BEN forms with corresponding payment methods such as PayPal, Xoom, Remitly, or cryptocurrency wallets). By integrating blockchain-based accounting for crypto transactions, the platform maintains transparent and verifiable records of who was paid, when payments occurred, and through which method, ensuring clear audit trails. Users can upload large batches of files (e.g., 50+ documents) and interact with the AI through prompts to ask questions, validate data, and initiate automated processing. The solution supports multiple tax entity types—including individuals, sole proprietorships, LLCs, S-Corporations, C-Corporations, and charities—while initially focusing on compliance with tax rules in New Hampshire (USA) and Cavite (Philippines), with the flexibility to expand to other jurisdictions.
