Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CryptArtist Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the CryptArtist Studio desktop application ("the App") and our associated websites, including cryptartist.com, mattyjacks.com, and givegigs.com (collectively, "the Services").

CryptArtist Studio is operated from the State of New Hampshire, United States of America. By using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

For questions about this policy, contact us at Matt@MattyJacks.com or visit MattyJacks.com/Contact.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact information: If you email us or use our contact forms, we collect your name, email address, and message content.
  • API keys: You may enter API keys for third-party services (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Pexels, etc.) into the App. These keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Donation information: If you make a voluntary donation through mattyjacks.com or givegigs.com, payment information is processed by our third-party payment processors. We do not store credit card numbers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Website analytics: Our websites use Google Analytics and Vercel observability tools to collect anonymized usage data, including page views, referral sources, browser type, and general geographic location.
  • Cloudflare: Our websites are served through Cloudflare, which may collect IP addresses and request metadata for security and performance purposes, in accordance with Cloudflare's own privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: We use Cloudflare Turnstile for bot protection on forms. This service may collect device and browser characteristics to verify you are human.
  • Desktop application: The CryptArtist Studio desktop application does not collect or transmit any personal data, usage data, or telemetry to our servers unless you explicitly opt in to a future analytics feature. The App runs entirely on your local machine.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

When you use AI-powered features in the App, your prompts and file contents may be sent to the AI provider you have configured (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). These transmissions are made directly from your device to the provider's API. We do not act as an intermediary and do not have access to this data. Please review the privacy policies of the respective AI providers for information about how they handle your data.

Similarly, when you use Pexels media search or GiveGigs media bucket features, requests are made directly from your device to those services.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To process voluntary donations.
  • To improve our websites and services through anonymized analytics.
  • To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use third-party services (Vercel, Cloudflare, Google Analytics, payment processors) that may process data on our behalf, subject to their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • With your consent: We may share information when you have given us explicit permission to do so.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your project files, API keys, and application settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. We implement reasonable security measures to protect information collected through our websites, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser privacy features or ad blockers.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at Matt@MattyJacks.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Links

The Services may contain links to third-party websites, including mattyjacks.com, givegigs.com, sitefari.com, github.com, and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our websites may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and functionality. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. The desktop application does not use tracking cookies.

10. Services We Use

The following third-party services are used across our platforms:

  • Vercel: Website hosting and observability tools.
  • Cloudflare: CDN, security, and DNS services.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: Bot protection for web forms.
  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics.
  • OpenAI API: AI features (user-configured, user's own API key).
  • Our own analytics suite: Additional website analytics for performance monitoring.

11. New Hampshire Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New Hampshire, United States of America. CryptArtist Studio complies with applicable New Hampshire state privacy and consumer protection laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact
